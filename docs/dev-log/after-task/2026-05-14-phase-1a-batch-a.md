## What did not go smoothly

- **Two NS-1..NS-5 stragglers** (`DESCRIPTION` and
  `vignettes/gllvmTMB.Rmd`) were not caught by the NS-4
  sweep because I scoped NS-4 to `vignettes/articles/`
  only. The top-level `vignettes/gllvmTMB.Rmd` is the Get
  Started vignette and lives outside that pattern.
  Similarly, `DESCRIPTION` was outside the scope grep.
  **Lesson**: when sweeping math notation across the
  package, the scope grep should include `DESCRIPTION`,
  `vignettes/*.Rmd` (top-level), `inst/CITATION`, and
  `inst/COPYRIGHTS` explicitly, not just
  `vignettes/articles/`. Updating the check-log Kaizen
  protocol to add this enumeration.
