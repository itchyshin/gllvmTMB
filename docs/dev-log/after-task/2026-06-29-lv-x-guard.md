## 1. Goal

Protect the Design 73 C1 claim boundary for predictor-informed latent scores.
The admitted C1 route is ordinary unit-tier `latent(..., lv = ~ x)` without an
ordinary fixed-effect covariate design beside it. Formulas that combine
fixed-effect RHS covariates with predictor-informed LV score means must fail
loudly until the combined `X + X_lv` regime has its own derivation, recovery
tests, and interval evidence.

## 2. Implemented

- `gll_prepare_lv_predictor_setup()` now rejects any fixed-effect RHS covariate
  when a predictor-informed `lv` term is present.
- The guard covers exact overlap such as
  `x + latent(..., lv = ~ x)` and non-overlap such as
  `z + latent(..., lv = ~ x)`.
- The error names the fixed-effect RHS columns and points to the gated
  `X + X_lv` regime.
- `test-lv-parser-guard.R` now checks both the overlap and non-overlap failure
  paths.
- NEWS, Design 73, the validation-debt register, and capability status now say
  the boundary is fail-loud only and does not admit combined `X + X_lv` fits.

## 3. Mathematical Contract

No likelihood, TMB parameterisation, family, NAMESPACE, exported function, Rd,
vignette, or pkgdown navigation changed.

The supported Design 73 C1 score-mean form remains:

```text
u_i = X_lv,i alpha + e_i,    e_i ~ N(0, I_K)
B_lv = Lambda alpha'
```

This slice does not implement the combined mean model
`eta = X beta + Lambda X_lv alpha + Lambda e`. It rejects that surface before
fitting because the fixed-effect and latent-score mean contributions need a
separate identifiability derivation and recovery evidence.

## 4a. Decisions and Rejected Alternatives

Decision: reject all ordinary fixed-effect RHS covariates beside
predictor-informed `lv`, not only exact overlap. Rationale: even non-overlap
`z + lv = ~ x` is part of the unvalidated combined `X + X_lv` regime, and the
current C1 evidence does not prove that fixed effects and latent-score means are
separately identifiable in that combined surface. Rejected alternative: allow
non-overlap covariates and reject only exact overlap. Confidence: high.

Decision: keep this as a parser/preflight guard, not a TMB-side numerical
guard. Rationale: the unsupported surface should fail before model setup rather
than relying on fit instability to expose the problem. Rejected alternative:
allow construction and inspect rank or Hessian diagnostics later. Confidence:
high.
